Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a joining fee?

Currently, we are offering zero joining fees on all memberships! This is a limited-time promotion.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or travel. A small administrative fee applies.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No long-term contracts required.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with valid ID receive 20% off all membership plans.

Can I bring a guest?

Premium and Elite members receive guest passes. Basic members can purchase day passes for guests at $15 each.

What if I want to upgrade?

You can upgrade your membership at any time. The difference will be pro-rated for the current month.
