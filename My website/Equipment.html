Premium Equipment

Industry-leading fitness equipment for optimal results

State-of-the-Art Fitness Technology

We invest in the best equipment to ensure you have the most effective and enjoyable workout experience

Cardio Equipment

Treadmills

20 premium treadmills with personal entertainment screens, cushioned running surfaces, and advanced monitoring systems. Features include interval training programs, heart rate monitoring, and virtual running courses.

Exercise Bikes

15 stationary bikes including upright and recumbent models. Each bike features adjustable resistance, comfortable seating, and built-in workout programs for all fitness levels.

Rowing Machines

8 concept rowers providing low-impact, full-body cardio workouts. Perfect for building endurance and burning calories while minimizing joint stress.

Elliptical Trainers

12 cross-trainers with adjustable stride length and incline settings. Ideal for low-impact cardio that engages both upper and lower body muscles.

Strength Training Equipment

Free Weights Zone

Complete range of dumbbells from 2kg to 50kg, Olympic barbells, EZ curl bars, and weight plates. Multiple bench press stations, squat racks, and deadlift platforms available.

Cable Machines

10 dual-adjustable cable stations with various attachments for targeting every muscle group. Smooth pulley systems and weight stacks from 5kg to 100kg.

Resistance Machines

Over 30 plate-loaded and selectorized machines covering all major muscle groups: chest press, shoulder press, lat pulldown, leg press, leg curl, and more.

Smith Machines

3 smith machines for controlled strength training. Perfect for beginners learning form or advanced lifters performing heavy sets safely.

Functional Training Equipment

Functional Training Zone

TRX suspension trainers, battle ropes, medicine balls (2kg-10kg), slam balls, resistance bands, and agility ladders for dynamic, full-body workouts.

Kettlebells

Complete set of kettlebells ranging from 4kg to 32kg. Perfect for swings, snatches, and Turkish get-ups to build functional strength and power.

Plyometric Equipment

Plyo boxes (various heights), jump ropes, hurdles, and agility cones for explosive power training and athletic conditioning.

Stretching & Recovery

Dedicated stretching area with yoga mats, foam rollers, massage balls, and stretching straps. Essential for warm-up, cool-down, and injury prevention.

Regular Maintenance

All equipment is inspected and maintained weekly to ensure optimal performance and safety. Any issues are addressed immediately.

Equipment Tutorials

Free orientation sessions available for all new members. Our trainers will show you proper form and technique on all equipment.

Hygiene Standards

Cleaning stations throughout the gym with antibacterial wipes and spray. Equipment is deep-cleaned daily for your safety.
