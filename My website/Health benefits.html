Why Exercise and Healthy Living Matter

Regular physical activity and a healthy lifestyle are among the most important things you can do for your health. The benefits extend far beyond weight management and physical appearance, affecting every aspect of your wellbeing from mental health to disease prevention.

Exercise Recommendations

Adults (18-64 years)

  • At least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity per week
  • Or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity
  • Muscle-strengthening activities 2 or more days per week
  • Mix of cardio and strength training for optimal results

Older Adults (65+ years)

  • Same as adults, adapted to fitness level
  • Activities that improve balance 3 or more days per week
  • Flexibility and stretching exercises daily
  • Consult healthcare provider before starting new programs

Getting Started

  • Start slowly and gradually increase intensity
  • Choose activities you enjoy to stay motivated
  • Set realistic, achievable goals
  • Consider working with a personal trainer
