Our Facilities

World-class amenities for your complete fitness journey

State-of-the-Art Gym

Our 15,000 square foot gym floor features the latest equipment from industry-leading manufacturers. With separate zones for cardio, strength training, free weights, and functional fitness, you will have everything you need for a complete workout.

  • ✓ 50+ cardio machines with personal entertainment screens
  • ✓ Complete range of resistance machines
  • ✓ Extensive free weights area with Olympic platforms
  • ✓ Functional training zone with TRX, kettlebells, and battle ropes
  • ✓ Air-conditioned environment with premium sound system
  • ✓ Personal training services available

Olympic Swimming Pool

Our 25-meter heated pool is perfect for lap swimming, aqua aerobics, or family fun. With dedicated lanes for different skill levels and qualified lifeguards on duty, everyone can enjoy the water safely.

  • ✓ 6-lane 25-meter heated pool
  • ✓ Separate children's pool area
  • ✓ Swimming lessons for all ages
  • ✓ Aqua aerobics classes
  • ✓ Certified lifeguards on duty
  • ✓ Pool-side changing rooms and showers

Tennis & Squash Courts

Enjoy our professional-grade tennis and squash facilities. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned player, our courts provide the perfect environment for your game.

  • ✓ 4 outdoor tennis courts with professional surfaces
  • ✓ 2 indoor squash courts with glass back walls
  • ✓ Court booking system for convenience
  • ✓ Professional coaching available
  • ✓ Equipment rental service
  • ✓ League and tournament opportunities

Indoor Sports Hall

Our multi-purpose indoor sports hall can accommodate basketball, volleyball, badminton, indoor football, and more. Perfect for team sports, group activities, and special events.

  • ✓ Full-size basketball court
  • ✓ Volleyball court setup available
  • ✓ 4 badminton courts
  • ✓ Indoor 5-a-side football
  • ✓ Spectator seating area
  • ✓ Available for private bookings

Sauna & Wellness Area

Relax and recover after your workout in our premium sauna and steam rooms. Our wellness area provides the perfect environment for muscle recovery and relaxation.

  • ✓ Finnish dry sauna
  • ✓ Steam room with aromatherapy
  • ✓ Relaxation lounge
  • ✓ Separate male and female facilities
  • ✓ Cold plunge pool
  • ✓ Premium changing facilities
