Class Information

How to Book

Premium and Elite members can book classes through our mobile app or at reception. Classes are bookable up to 7 days in advance. Basic members can purchase class passes.

What to Bring

Bring a water bottle, towel, and appropriate footwear. Yoga mats, equipment, and weights are provided for all classes.

Cancellation Policy

Please cancel at least 2 hours before class start time. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in class credits being deducted.

New to Classes?

All our instructors are happy to help beginners. Arrive 5-10 minutes early to introduce yourself and discuss any concerns or modifications needed.
